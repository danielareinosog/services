The People Systems Scan

Three weeks to see your organization clearly.

A focused diagnostic for growing, mission-driven organizations that need to understand what's creating people-ops friction — and what to fix first.

Is This You?

The Scan is built for organizations where…

  • Your ED, COO, or ops lead has become the default owner of every people question.
  • Hiring, onboarding, performance, or compensation works differently depending on who handles it.
  • Your values are clear on paper, but harder to translate into daily decisions.
  • You have payroll and admin covered — but no one owns the people system.
  • One conflict, underperformance issue, or messy exit has revealed how much is unwritten.

Or maybe things feel mostly fine — and you're not sure whether that's true, or whether no one's had time to look.

Take the 3-Minute Snapshot
What It Is

A map of what's breaking, and a plan for what to fix first.

The Scan shows you where your people systems are creating drag, risk, or confusion. We identify the recurring patterns, clarify the highest-priority gaps, and translate the findings into a practical 90-day stabilization roadmap your team can actually use.

Start With the Scan

You leave with

  • People systems gap map
  • Priority risk areas
  • Leadership debrief
  • 90-day stabilization roadmap
  • Recommendations for what to build, delegate, or defer
How It Works

Three weeks, three moves.

Week One

Listen + review

Kickoff with leadership, review core documents, and identify the gap between what the organization says and what is written down.

Week Two

Pulse + walkthrough

Speak with key stakeholders and walk through one high-friction workflow — hiring, onboarding, or performance support.

Week Three

Connect the dots

Analyze patterns, map the findings, and deliver the 90-day stabilization roadmap in a leadership debrief.
