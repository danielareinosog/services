A note on investment

My pricing is value-based and scope-based. The ranges below reflect typical investments for U.S.-based, global, and well-funded organizations. I also work with Ecuador-based and Latin America-based organizations, where pricing may be calibrated to local market context, organizational budget, and scope.

The goal is a fair engagement: one that reflects the value of the work, the complexity of the problem, and the realities of the organization.
