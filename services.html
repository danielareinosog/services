Services

Three ways we can work together.

Every engagement starts with seeing clearly. What happens next depends on what we find — and what your organization is ready for.

01 · Start Here

People Systems Scan

A focused three-week diagnostic. We map where your people systems are creating drag, risk, or confusion — and turn the findings into a 90-day plan for what to fix first.

Typical investment USD $3,500+

This is right for you if…

  • People-ops friction keeps recurring, and nobody's sure exactly why.
  • Leadership time is being pulled into preventable issues.
  • You want to understand what's happening before investing in building anything.

You leave with: a people systems gap map, priority risk areas, a leadership debrief, and a 90-day stabilization roadmap.

About the Scan→
02 · Then Build

People Systems Build

With priorities clear, we build the systems that matter most — designed for your stage, put in writing, and handed off to your team.

Typical investment USD $8,000–$20,000+

This is right for you if…

  • You know what's broken — through the Scan or hard experience.
  • You're ready to put hiring, onboarding, role clarity, or performance rhythms in writing.
  • You want systems your team will actually use, not a policy binder.

You leave with: working systems for your highest priorities — hiring, onboarding, role clarity, performance rhythms, manager support, policy foundations — plus implementation support and a clean handoff.

Let's talk→
03 · Then Sustain

Ongoing Advisory

Steady senior counsel as you implement, make people decisions, and lead through change — without me becoming the person everything lands on.

Typical investment USD $2,500–$6,000/mo

This is right for you if…

  • You're implementing new systems and want experienced eyes along the way.
  • People decisions keep escalating to you, and you want a thinking partner.
  • You need senior people & culture judgment without a full-time hire.

You get: ongoing implementation support, leadership advisory, and help with team friction and culture work — at a cadence that fits your organization.

A Note on Investment

My pricing is value-based and scope-based. The ranges above reflect typical investments for U.S.-based, global, and well-funded organizations. I also work with Ecuador-based and Latin America-based organizations, where pricing is calibrated to local market context, organizational budget, and scope.

The goal is a fair engagement: one that reflects the value of the work, the complexity of the problem, and the realities of the organization.

"The right engagement is the one your organization is actually ready for."

Not sure which that is? That's exactly what a first conversation is for.
