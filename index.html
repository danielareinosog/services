Phase 1: The Chaos-to-Clarity Scan

We stop the bleeding in 3 weeks. Here's how.

↓

The Promise

You feel the chaos—delayed decisions, unclear policies, or sudden vacancies—but you don't know the root cause. This diagnostic is a low-risk, high-speed engagement to map exactly where your organization is vulnerable and what to fix first.

The 3-Week Roadmap

Week 1: Discovery & Listening
  • Review of existing handbooks, contracts, and recent exit interview data
  • 1:1 Confidential interviews with key leadership and culture carriers
  • Goal: Understand the "shadow culture" vs. the written rules
Week 2: Analysis & Mapping
  • Identifying compliance risks (labor laws, safeguarding gaps)
  • Mapping the decision-making bottlenecks
  • Drafting the "Chaos Map" visualization
Week 3: The Action Plan
  • Finalize prioritized 90-day stabilization plan
  • 60-minute leadership strategy session
  • Align on next steps

The Deliverable

A prioritized 90-day stabilization plan with 3-5 "no-regrets" actions you can take immediately.

A 60-minute strategy session to align leadership on the path forward.

Outcome: You stop guessing. You get a clear, visual map of your risks and a literal checklist to stabilize the ship.

Phase 2: The People Systems Build

Turning your values into simple, repeatable systems.

↓

The Philosophy: "Good Enough" Governance

We do not copy-paste corporate bureaucracy. We build "minimum viable policies" that protect your organization without crushing your soul. We focus on the systems that actually matter for global, mission-driven teams.

Sample Menu: What We Might Build

Based on the Chaos Map, we select 3-5 core systems to implement:

Global Remote Work Policy Fair compensation and compliance across borders.
Safeguarding & Ethics Clear reporting lines for investigations and grievances.
Performance "Lite" A feedback rhythm that helps staff grow without complex forms.
Equitable Hiring Guide Reducing bias in recruitment before it starts.

The "Stickiness" Factor

Most consultants hand you a PDF and leave. I stay to ensure adoption:

Documentation We write the policies in plain, human language.
Manager Training I run 1-2 live workshops to teach your leaders how to use the new tools.
Implementation We roll it out together, answering staff questions in real-time.
