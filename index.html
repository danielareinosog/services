Hi, I'm Daniela

I've built these systems inside the organizations that needed them.

For over a decade I led people and culture work across nonprofits and international NGOs — including building the people function from scratch at an international human rights organization, supporting teams across 13 countries. I started this practice because I kept seeing capable, mission-driven teams held back by the same structural gaps, and leaders carrying weight a well-designed system should carry instead.

My point of view is simple: structure is not bureaucracy. Done well, it's what protects the mission — and the people doing the work.
