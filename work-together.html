Work Together

It starts with one conversation.

No pitch. A clear read on what's happening in your organization, and an honest answer about whether I can help.

What to Expect

Three steps, no pressure.

  • 01

    A 30-minute conversation

    You talk, I listen. We walk through what's creating drag, risk, or confusion in your people systems, and what kind of support would actually be useful.

  • 02

    A clear read

    I'll tell you what I'm seeing — including whether this is something to solve now, something to watch, or something you can handle internally.

  • 03

    A proposal, only if it fits

    If there's a fit, I'll send a short proposal with clear scope, timeline, and investment. If there isn't, I'll say so — and point you somewhere useful.

Who I Work With

Mission-driven organizations in a season of growth.

Most of my clients are nonprofits, NGOs, and social enterprises with fewer than 100 staff — organizations where the mission is strong, the team is committed, and the people systems haven't caught up to either. Usually there's no dedicated people function, and a founder, ED, or ops lead is carrying it.

If that sounds like your organization, we should talk.

Book a conversation.

Thirty minutes, no pitch. If I can help, you'll know exactly how. If I can't, you'll leave with a clearer picture anyway.
